About the band

Ne Obliviscaris is a critically acclaimed Australian progressive metal band formed in 2003 that has toured worldwide with giant names in the scene, such as Devin Townsend Project, Enslaved, Soilwork, Cradle of Filth and more. Favoring longer form compositions and relying on a masterful combination of both highly technical and devilishly simple rhythmic components.
